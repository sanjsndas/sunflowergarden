Cookies Policy

Effective date: June 2024

At Sunflower Garden Pruning, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand your preferences, improve site functionality, and analyze usage patterns to provide better services.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer or mobile device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and provide information to the owners.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the operation of our website, enabling features such as page navigation and secure areas access.
  • Performance Cookies: These collect anonymous data on how users interact with our website, helping us improve functionality and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: These remember your preferences, like language or region, to personalize your experience.
  • Third-Party Cookies: We may use external services such as analytics providers who place cookies to collect visitor information in an anonymous way.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us:

  • Recognize your device during visits.
  • Remember your site preferences.
  • Understand how you use our website and improve its performance.
  • Ensure security and detect potential threats.

Your Control Over Cookies

You can control or disable cookies using your browser settings. Depending on your browser, you may block third-party cookies, clear stored cookies, or restrict cookies to certain websites.

Please note that disabling cookies may impact your experience and certain parts of the website may not function properly.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner asking you to accept or decline the usage of cookies. Your choice is stored in your browser for future visits.
