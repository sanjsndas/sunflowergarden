Privacy Policy

At Sunflower Garden Pruning, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you share when using our services or visiting our website.

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure; however, no internet system is completely secure. We recommend you do not share sensitive information through unsecured channels.

If you have any questions, requests regarding your data, or would like to opt out of communications, please contact us. We value your trust and strive to maintain transparency and integrity in managing your privacy.
